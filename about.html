OUR STORY

The Sergiani Seaside is a family-owned business with deep roots in the tradition of fresh fish. For years, our family has been dedicated to providing the best quality from the sea to your plate through our own fish market. At our restaurant, however, you will discover much more than just fish and seafood. Our menu embraces Greek and Mediterranean cuisine in all its richness: selected meats, pasta, seasonal vegetables, and fresh greens, all cooked with love and served with care. Complete your meal with a carefully curated list of fine Greek wines, while enjoying the atmosphere of the seaside promenade of Agios Nikolaos. Right by the water, with a view of the picturesque harbor, Sergiani Seaside invites you to experience authentic hospitality and true taste.

OUR TEAM

At the heart of our restaurant is a passionate team dedicated to creating unforgettable dining experiences. From our talented chefs who craft every dish with skill and creativity, to our attentive waiters who serve each guest with warmth and professionalism, every member of our team plays an important role. Working together like a family, we share a commitment to quality, hospitality, and genuine care, making sure that every visit feels special.
